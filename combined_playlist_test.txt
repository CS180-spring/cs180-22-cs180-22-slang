Objective: 
	- To verify the final playlist of two or more combined playlists does not have any extra or missing song.

Preconditions: 
	- Two or more playlists that are ready to be combined.
	- The user must input URLs of the playlists they want to combine.

Expected Outcomes: 
	- No extra songs are added to the final playlist.
	- Duplicate songs should be removed.
	- The number of songs in the final playlist should be less than or equal to the sum of all songs in the inputted playlists.

Test Results: 
	- A single playlists that has all unique songs from the inputted playlists.
