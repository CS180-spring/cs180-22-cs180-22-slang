
Objective:
To verify that the combined playlist has an even distribution of songs from each individual playlist.

Preconditions:
Two or more playlists are available to be combined.
The user has selected the playlists to be combined.

Expected Outcomes:

The combined playlist should be properly constructed.
The estimated amount of songs should be present on each separate playlist.
In the combined playlist, the music distribution should be balanced.
The percentage variation must be below or equal to the designated threshold value.
Different playlist combinations should pass the test.

Test Results
Two or more playlists with different numbers of songs.
